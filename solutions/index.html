ERRCS - Emergency Responder Radio Coverage Systems

Since 9/11, agencies such as the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) and International Fire Code (IFC) worked to improve occupant and first responder safety in buildings.

Federal regulators mandated ERRCS under their compliance requirements for building occupancy rules.

These rules are also developed and enhanced on both State and County levels with specific requirements for each area’s needs and considerations.

  • Pre-Construction Consulting
  • Benchmark RF Grid Testing
  • ERRCS Design and Permitting
  • RF Engineering
  • Recertification and Maintenance
  • ERRCS Installation
  • Remote Monitoring and Optimization
