Who We Are

Wireless Carrier DAS

DAS System solves your cell coverage issues by delivering mission-critical communication, through in-building Distributed Antenna Systems, or DAS for short.

Contact Us
Pre-Construction Consulting

We provide value-driven workarounds for any communication challenges. Our engineers can find a way to get through by assessing factors affecting your communications.

Our team can help render pre-construction predictive models, design plans and coverage areas, and cover any gaps.

RF Spot Testing

We perform benchmark radio frequency (RF) grid tests. We also assess the grid and frequency data to identify coverage deficiencies throughout the area.

Wireless Carrier DAS Design

Our team of FCC Licensed Level 3 iBwave Certified RF Designers provides design-conscious solutions for your wireless carrier system needs.

Remote Monitoring and Optimization

Whether you own a small business or a Fortune 500 company, DAS Systems have to be maintained and serviced yearly.

By deploying a leading remote monitoring solution, we help you maintain optimal visibility and periodic optimization over the network, while reducing resource deployment costs.

RF Engineering

Our DAS Systems engineers utilize cutting edge technologies and methodologies to RF engineer, design, develop, install and maintain your cellular DAS needs

DAS Installation

Our design-oriented Wi-Fi Systems installations are aesthetically and technically matched with your building’s design, delivering a performance-oriented design. 

DAS System’s attention to detail ensures that the end product is a unique system that takes the user and owner’s experiences to the next level.

Maintenance and Recertification

Whether you own a small business or a Fortune 500 company, DAS Systems have to be maintained and serviced yearly. 

Annual testing and recertification is highly recommended for Distributed Antenna Systems to ensure your internet coverage remains at its peak.
