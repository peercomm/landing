Who is DAS Systems

Who is DAS Systems

Since 2002, we’ve dedicated our lives to providing life-saving communications and safety solutions, meeting both business and commercial needs. Our team leverages its experience, know-how and agility to deliver products that optimize businesses, improve building safety and enhance the customer experience. 

Communication saves lives, and an adequate communications system is the foundation of a modern, functional and safe atmosphere. Contact our team of expert engineers to see how we can help you value engineer your building and take your business to the next level. 

Message from Our CEO

Since 2002, we’ve embarked on a journey to bring next-level technology and safety systems to our clients.

Our team has grown exponentially, and we’ve fostered new partnerships and opportunities along the way, in cities nationwide.

Over the years, we’ve been offered continuous learning opportunities through our clients, helping us further reach our vision of closing the loop on life safety systems, as a company.

The DAS Systems team is always on the move, driving our enterprise forward as a functional unit and constantly seeking value-driven results, experience, and meaningful partnerships.

The History of

DAS SYSTEMS

2002
We started Dishnet Direct, offering low voltage systems, essential satellite network designs, head-end systems, operating out of California.
2011
We expanded our services to include ERRCS as part of a growing need in the real estate market
2013

A decade after starting, we broadened our horizon and set out to offer wireless carrier DAS solutions

2015
DAS Systems was created with a mission to focus on RF engineering, design, ERRCS, carrier DAS and Wi-Fi systems, operating as a subsidiary of Dishnet Direct.
2017
We offered our services to our 100th partner
2019
Our partnerships grew, and our network expanded, as we set out to deliver our 200th project.
2020
New sights were set, and our second office opened out to service partners out of Colorado
2021
We completed the handover of our 250th project and expanded our presence with our new office in Texas.
