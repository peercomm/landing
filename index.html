01

ERRCS - Emergency Responder Radio Coverage Systems

Since 9/11, agencies such as the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) and the International Fire Code (IFC) worked to improve occupant and first responder safety in buildings.

Federal regulators started mandating ERRCS under their compliance requirements for building occupancy rules.

DAS Systems offers turnkey ERRCS installations, fire alarms and two-way communications systems.

These rules are also constantly reviewed on both State and County levels with specific requirements for each area’s current needs and considerations.

Learn More
02

Wireless Carrier DAS

Indoor and outdoor network enhancements are accomplished through multiple technical topologies (i.e., Repeaters, Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS), Wi-Fi, Carrier DAS, and more).

DAS Systems solves your cell coverage issues by delivering mission-critical communication through Distributed Antenna Systems, or DAS for short.

A DAS system allows you to improve your overall buildings experience by providing users with increased coverage, internet access, efficiency, and safety through reliable network access.
